✦ The Concept

What's a Light RPG
Coloring Book?

It's a coloring book where every color you choose secretly matters. No dice. No rulebook. Just your creative instincts guiding a story toward one of several unique endings.

🎨

Color Freely

Choose any colors you love — each palette family secretly influences your character's hidden stats as you play.

⚔️

Build Hidden Stats

Three invisible forces — Grace, Shadow, and Fate — shift with every choice. You won't know exactly where you stand until the end.

📖

Story That Responds

Chapters unfold like illustrated storybooks. Your color choices shape the narrative and unlock different scenes.

🌟

Multiple Endings

Replay and recolor to discover every path. Each ending rewards a completely different creative approach.

The Dragon of Thornfield

In the kingdom of Thornvale, a dragon stirs beneath the ancient mountain. You are Elara — and how you color your journey determines whether Varathos becomes destroyer, ally, or legend.

  • 6 illustrated chapters to color
  • 5 unique characters with storylines
  • 3 stat paths: Grace · Shadow · Fate
  • 3 branching endings to unlock
  • Playable digitally (WebGL) or as PDF
