✦ About This Space

What is
Coloring Worlds?

Coloring Worlds is a solo indie creative studio building a library of Light RPG Digital Coloring Books — interactive stories where the colors you choose secretly shape the ending.

"I'm retiring in a few years and I want to spend that time building magical worlds I genuinely love. This is that journey."
— Christine, Founder
🎨
Light RPG Coloring Books Color choices secretly build hidden stats that determine your ending. No dice. No rules. Just color.

✦ The Worlds

The Coloring Worlds
Library

Three unique worlds. Three different tones. One shared mechanic — where your colors write the story.

🏰
Kingdoms of Color
Hero: Elara · The Dragon of Thornfield
✦ Grace ◆ Shadow ● Fate

A gothic medieval fantasy. In the kingdom of Thornvale, a dragon stirs. Elara's color choices determine whether Varathos becomes destroyer, ally, or legend.

🌊
Tides of Aethermoor
Hero: Nerissa · The Mermaid Between Worlds
🌊 Sea ☁️ Sky 🌿 Shore

A romantic underwater adventure. Nerissa is torn between her glittering kingdom and the world above — while the ancient witch Morvaine threatens everything.

The Enchanted Apothecary
Hero: Pip · The Tangle Root Apothecary
🥄 Stir ✨ Spark 💧 Spill

A whimsical forest comedy. Pip accidentally made Thistle the mouse very smart and very talkative. Three important customers arrive before sundown. Chaos ensues.
